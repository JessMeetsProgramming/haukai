Privacy Statement

We collect personal information from you, including information about your:

  • name
  • contact information
  • interactions with us

We collect your personal information in order to:

  • provide online booking services to our customers and to be able to contact them regarding the services they are requesting.
  • track how users interact with our website to improve the user experience and to provide further functionality to The Haukai Restaurant customers and staff.

Providing some information is optional:

  • If you choose not to enter name and contact details, we'll be unable to confirm table reservations or respond to queries.
  • If you choose not to allow cookies we'll be unable to use analytics to provide a more tailored visitor experience.

You have the right to ask for a copy of any personal information we hold about you, and to ask for it to be corrected if you think it is wrong.

If you'd like to ask for a copy of your information, or to have it corrected, please contact us at haukairestaurant@gmail.com, or (09) 401 4019.
