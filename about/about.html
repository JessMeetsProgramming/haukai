The Owners

Haukai Restaurant specialises in Maori kai and is based in the Bay of Islands. It was started by a couple who are keen on creating a business that would appeal to locals and tourists visiting the area. Proud of their Maori heritage and wanting to be a part of the revival of the Maori language and culture they saw an opportunity to share their knowledge through kai.

Our Culture

We want customers to experience Te Ao Maori with the use of karakia for blessing food. We uphold tikanga Maori in everything we do. We look to our ancestors hunting and foraging customs and use locally grown and sourced ingredients that were traditionally eaten before European foods were introduced.
